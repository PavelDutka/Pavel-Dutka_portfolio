My Story

Hello! I'm Pavel Dutka, a multidisciplinary creative professional specializing in 3D visualization and software development. With over 8 years of experience in the digital creation field, I've developed a unique skill set that bridges the gap between visual artistry and technical implementation.

My journey began in 3D modeling and visualization, where I honed my skills creating detailed architectural renderings and product visualizations. As I delved deeper into the technical aspects of 3D work, I discovered a passion for programming and automation that could streamline creative workflows.

Today, I combine my artistic eye with technical expertise to deliver comprehensive solutions for clients across various industries. Whether it's developing custom web applications, creating photorealistic 3D visualizations, or building automation tools to save time and resources, I approach each project with attention to detail and a commitment to excellence.
